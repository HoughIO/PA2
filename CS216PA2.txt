Welcome to my text editor.
To insert text at the end of the file, type a line and press enter.
To insert text at a certain line number, type 'I' followed by a space and the desired line number.
To delete a line, type 'D' followed by a space and the line number.
To print all the lines, type 'L' and press enter.
To exit, type 'E' and press enter.
To display this introduction, type 'H' and press enter.
